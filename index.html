About Us

Akhilesh GPS - Jaipur's Most Trusted GPS Tracker

Leading GPS vehicle tracking company in Rajasthan — trusted by 5000+ vehicles

Akhilesh GPS is Jaipur's most trusted GPS vehicle tracking company. We provide real-time vehicle monitoring solutions for transport companies, logistics firms, school buses, taxi fleets, and personal vehicles across Rajasthan and India. We currently have over 5,000 active vehicles being tracked on our platform.

Rising fuel costs, driver negligence, and vehicle theft are serious challenges that our efficient GPS tracking system solves. Akhilesh GPS has built a solution that is both affordable and powerful for small and large fleet owners across Jaipur and Rajasthan.

Setting up is extremely simple. Once the GPS device is installed in your vehicle, you can instantly view your vehicle's live location from our web dashboard or mobile app — from anywhere, anytime.

Our Story: Akhilesh GPS was born from a simple vision — every fleet owner in Jaipur deserves complete control over their vehicles, whether they have 2 trucks or 200. We answered all fleet management challenges in one single platform.

Real-Time Tracking: Our GPS device updates vehicle location every 10 seconds. You'll know exactly where your driver is, at what speed they're travelling, and whether the route is correct.

Geo-Fencing: Create a custom virtual boundary on the map. If your vehicle crosses it, you instantly receive an SMS and app notification. Perfect for school buses and transport companies in Jaipur.

Save Fuel, Save Money: Reduce fuel costs by 15-20% on average. With 10 trucks spending Rs.20,000 on fuel monthly, you could save Rs.30,000-40,000 every single month.

Driver Behavior Monitoring: Hard braking, sharp turning, overspeeding — all events recorded in detailed reports helping you improve safety and reduce maintenance costs.

Remote Immobilizer: If your vehicle is stolen, remotely shut down the engine with a single tap and share live location with police instantly.

Why Choose Akhilesh GPS? We don't just sell a GPS device — we become your business partner. Certified technicians handle installation across Jaipur and Rajasthan, support is 24/7, and our dashboard is designed so anyone can use it without technical knowledge.
