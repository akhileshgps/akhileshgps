About Us

Akhilesh GPS - India's Most Trusted GPS Partner

Discover why thousands of fleet owners trust us every day

Akhilesh GPS is one of India's leading GPS tracking companies providing real-time vehicle monitoring for transport companies, logistics firms, school buses, taxi fleets, and personal vehicles. We have over 5,000 active vehicles on our platform and that number grows every day.

In today's digital age, fleet management is a necessity. Rising fuel costs, driver negligence, and vehicle theft are challenges an efficient GPS system can solve. Akhilesh GPS has built a solution that is both affordable and powerful for businesses across India.

Setting up is extremely simple. Once the GPS device is installed, you can view your vehicle's live location from our web dashboard or mobile app - whether you're at home, in the office, or in another city.

Our Story: Akhilesh GPS was born from a simple vision - every fleet owner deserves complete control over their vehicles, whether they have 2 trucks or 200. We answered all fleet management challenges in one single platform.

Real-Time Tracking: Our device updates location every 10 seconds. You'll know exactly where your driver is, at what speed they're travelling, and whether the route is correct.

Geo-Fencing: Create a custom virtual boundary on the map. If your vehicle crosses it, you instantly receive an SMS and app notification. Perfect for school buses and transport companies.

Save Fuel, Save Money: Reduce fuel costs by 15-20% on average. With 10 trucks spending Rs.20,000 on fuel monthly, you could save Rs.30,000-40,000 every single month.

Driver Behavior: Hard braking, sharp turning, overspeeding - all events recorded in detailed reports helping you improve safety and reduce maintenance costs.

Remote Immobilizer: If your vehicle is stolen, remotely shut down the engine with a single tap and share the live location with police instantly.

Why Choose Us? We don't just sell a GPS device - we become your business partner. Certified technicians handle installation, support is 24/7, and our dashboard is designed so anyone can use it without technical knowledge.
